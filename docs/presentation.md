## Slide 2: Problem & Opportunity
**The Challenge**
*   **Unplanned Downtime**: Unexpected breakdowns cost time and money.
*   **Reactive Maintenance**: Service is often too late or unnecessary.
*   **Disconnected Systems**: Manufacturing doesn't learn from service data.

**The Opportunity**
*   Proactive, data-driven maintenance.
*   Seamless, autonomous scheduling.
*   Continuous quality improvement loop.
